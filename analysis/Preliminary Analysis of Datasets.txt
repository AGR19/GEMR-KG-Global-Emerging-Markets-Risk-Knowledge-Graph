5. CONCLUSION\
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------\
\
The IFC_GEM.csv and Excel files do NOT share common ECONOMIC indicators.\
\
However, they share TWO critical dimensions for data integration:\
\
1. COUNTRY/REGION NAME - Allows geographic matching\
2. TIME PERIOD (Year) - Allows temporal matching\
\
These shared dimensions enable analysts to:\
- Link credit risk data (from IFC_GEM) with macro-economic indicators (from Excel files)\
- Perform country-year level analysis\
- Study correlations between economic conditions and default/recovery rates\
- Build predictive models using both credit and economic data\
\
Example use case:\
